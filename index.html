Meet the team...

Four ex-Gameloft seasoned game developers just founded a new indie game development studio called Sama Sama which means "You are welcome" in Indonesian.

Sama Sama studios is based in Estonia – Europe’s "Silicon Valley" famous for its efficient E-economy initiatives, bubbling IT startup scene and an entrepreneur-friendly business legislation and low taxes.

Founding members are scattered around the globe (Argentina, Canada, Estonia and Indonesia) running a fully digital office maintains the global view for the company operating in highly competitive gaming industry.


About The SAMA SAMA GAMES project

We in Sama Sama believe that social doesn't mean to be connected all times through different screens, but totally the opposite! We go back to the origins: Social is to be close and to be able to share instantly the experience on a face-to-face environment. It’s because of this that we decide to call ourselves an “OFFLINE MULTIPLAYER MOBILE GAMES” company.

Let me guess what you are thinking: “What? Offline? And multiplayer? How is that even possible?”. Well, simply: By going back to the roots. Board and tabletop games are still highly famous all around the world, and people enjoys gathering around to play them, that’s the real social moment

And that’s exactly what we aim with our products: A short and agile gaming experiencing of different users on a single device, that will bring satisfaction to them not just because of the gaming experience itself, but because of the moment shared, that connection in real time and life, that spark that make us humans and that sometimes we tend to forget that it exists.

Let’s enjoy the moment!
